About Us

Ecolocks, an ISO-certified and state-recognized company incubated at IIT Mandi, stands at the forefront of innovation in construction materials. We specialize in high-quality paver blocks, interlocking tiles, and precast RCC benches. With a growing network of branches across India, we are committed to delivering superior materials and exceptional service. Our focus on market organization and business development ensures that we not only meet but exceed client expectations. Trust Ecolocks for top-tier products and cutting-edge solutions that enhance your projects with both durability and style.
